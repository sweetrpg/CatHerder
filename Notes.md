# Catnip

* Change training treats to use:
  * wheat -> catnip
  * bone -> fish
  * sugar
  * string
  * gunpowder -> rabbit meat (raw or cooked?)
* Causes cat to use "crazy" (see scared chicken) goal
* Grows in the wild
* Can be planted and grown
* Bundles

# Accessories

* Yarn
* Cat toy
* Bundle of catnip: sprig (small), ?? (medium), bouquet (large)
